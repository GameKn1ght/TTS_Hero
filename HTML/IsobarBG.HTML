Appearance David is handsome but unassuming in appearance, often seen in slacks and a lab coat. As Isobar, he wears a form-fitting costume of varying hues of blue, along with a domino mask to hide his identity.
Campaign Use
Personality/ Motivation David became a meteorologist to help people and better the world, and this outlook hasn't changed since becoming Isobar. He sees his powers as an opportunity to prevent Destroyer from using his invention while helping the Millennium City's citizens any way he can.
Powers/Tactics Isobar has the ability to manipulate atmospheric pressure in his vicinity. His powers are useful for pushing people around, but he's mindful of collateral damage, which sometimes limits his choices in combat.
Background/ History
David was a scientist on the Zeus project, a joint endeavor by both NOAA and Millennium City University. The project sought to create a balloon-deployed device which would control local weather patterns. When the takeover by Doctor Destroyer occurred, the project was co-opted, presumably to turn the device into a weapon. In the following months, David made sure the project suffered setbacks, stalling in the hopes that a new set of heroes would rise and overthrow Destroyer’s evil regime. And so, in one late-night lab session, he went a little too far, causing the device to melt down while also exposing himself to the machine’s weather-altering rays. As he was the only one near the “accident,” none of the others in the lab were affected, and no one is aware of the changes the device’s rays made to his physiology.

The exposure gifted David with amazing control over atmospheric pressure and has been using them to quietly help people in his off hours as Isobar.

Meanwhile, Destroyer has restarted the project, and David has resumed trying to make the project appear to be progressing while doing his best to secretly undermine it -- more carefully this time.
